## App Listing Copy

**App name:** Midpoint
**Short description (80 chars):**
Find the fair meeting spot between you and your friends.

**Full description (4000 chars):**

Midpoint helps groups of friends, family, or colleagues find a fair place to
meet — the midpoint between everyone's locations.

Whether you're planning a coffee catch-up, a lunch with out-of-town friends,
or a dinner across the city, Midpoint calculates the geographic midpoint of
your group and suggests nearby restaurants and venues that work for everyone.

KEY FEATURES

• Add participants by address, place, or your current location
• Automatically calculates the fair midpoint
• Recommends nearby coffee shops, lunch spots, and dinner restaurants
• Shows distance and travel time from each participant
• Save favorite spots for quick future planning
• Sign in with Google or Apple — your data syncs across devices

WHY MIDPOINT?

Tired of one person always having to drive further? Midpoint takes the
negotiation out of "where should we meet?" by finding a spot that's fair for
everyone. It considers each participant's location and suggests venues that
minimize the longest travel time.

PRIVACY FIRST

We only collect what's needed to make the app work: your account info (for
sign-in), your location (when you search), and the spots you save. Full
details at https://midpoint-production-749a.up.railway.app/privacy
